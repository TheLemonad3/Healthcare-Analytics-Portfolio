Dashboard Spec – Provider Quality & Peer Review Console

Purpose
A single-page console to monitor provider compliance, OPPE performance signals, FPPE monitoring status, and peer review activity.

Primary Users
- Medical Staff / Credentialing Analysts
- Quality / Peer Review Teams
- Department Leadership (read-only)
- Providers (limited self-view, aggregated where appropriate)

Filters (Top)
- Department
- Specialty
- Time Window (30/90/180 days)
- Provider Status (Active / All)

KPI Tiles (Top Row)
- Total Providers
- % Credential Compliant
- Expiring in 30 Days
- % OPPE Below Threshold
- # Active FPPE
- Peer Review Cases (last 90 days)

Section A – Credential Risk (Left)
- Expirations by Department (bar)
- Expiring Soon Provider List (table)

Section B – OPPE Performance (Center)
- OPPE Score Distribution (histogram/bar)
- Avg OPPE Score by Department (bar)
- Flagged Providers (OPPE below threshold) (table)

Section C – Peer Review & FPPE (Right)
- FPPE Status Breakdown (donut)
- Peer Review Case Trend (line)
- Peer Review Volume by Department (bar)

Outputs to Capture for Portfolio
- Full dashboard screenshot
- Close-up of Expiring Soon table
- Close-up of OPPE distribution + flagged list
- Close-up of Peer Review trend + FPPE status
